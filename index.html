Bluesoxx hosts computer science programs for high school girls in Vancouver, BC.  This summer, we will take on new members with any experience level (including none), for our introductory course based on the Stanford University CS101 curriculum .   The session will begin late in Summer, 2019—be sure to sign up for the mailing list below to find out when registration opens!

No programming experience of any kind is required to join with Bluesoxx.

More About Bluesoxx

About Bluesoxx

First Class Ambitions

We will definitely make some fun and simple demo projects.   But overall, the class is about real fundamentals in formal computer science, and professional-level programming.   We will also work on leadership and presentation skills: less crafting-for-kids and more becoming-a-boss.

Getting it Done Together

The Bluesoxx program combines materials from professional-level computer science courses with personalized instruction by local experts.   Most importantly, though, students work together to build and share expertise as a team, and take the lead in making this happen: not quite like joining a sorority, but not just like taking a class.

Real Accomplishments

The ability to use software, like the ability to read 300 years ago, is transforming assumptions about what people can accomplish.   Early adopters who progress from computer-literate to best-selling-authors of computation are not just ensuring the income or lifestyle they want—they have the opportunity to impact the world.
